About the Artist

Hi, I am Katie. I live in Durham, North Carolina with my husband and our two dogs. I love texture, color, and patterns. I work with water colors, gouache, and block printing to create the style I want. I am constantly learning and exploring new techniques, mediums and different art forms. My life ambition is to never stop creating and growing.
